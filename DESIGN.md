# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic principles and technical specifications for our digital products.

## Theme Overview

Our theme is designed for a `light` color mode, ensuring readability and a vibrant user experience.

## Color Palette

The chosen colors establish a distinct brand identity:
* **Primary Color:** `#E2B331` - A vibrant and commanding shade, ideal for primary actions and brand highlights.
* **Secondary Color:** `#404040` - A deep, grounding tone that provides contrast and supports less prominent UI elements.
* **Tertiary Color:** `#F8F8F8` - A very light, almost white color, used for backgrounds, subtle accents, or where a bright, clean feel is desired.
* **Neutral Color:** `#111111` - A dark, strong neutral, providing depth and used for text and foundational elements.

## Typography

Our typography system combines classic elegance with modern legibility:
* **Headlines:** `notoSerif` - Chosen for its classic, serif appeal, providing a sophisticated touch to headings.
* **Body Text:** `plusJakartaSans` - A contemporary sans-serif, offering excellent readability for long-form content.
* **Labels:** `plusJakartaSans` - Consistent with body text for clarity and uniformity across interactive elements.

## Shape and Form

The interactive elements and containers in our design feature subtle roundedness (`1`), offering a soft yet defined appearance.

## Spacing and Layout

We implement a `normal` level of spacing (`2`), balancing density with sufficient whitespace for comfortable user interaction and visual hierarchy.
